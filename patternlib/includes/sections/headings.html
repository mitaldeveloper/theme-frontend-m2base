A Heading can also be styled to look like another heading by using one of the classes .h1 through .h6. For example:

I am a H1 Heading that looks like a H3 Heading

<h1 class="h3">I am a H1 Heading that looks like a H3 Heading</h1>

A heading can also be ‘decorated’ by adding a modifier class: .is-decorated. By default the decoration is black, but you can explicitly choose whether it is dark or light by using .is-decorated--dark or .is-decorated--light

This Is A Decorated Heading
And Subheading

<h3 class="is-decorated u-text-align--center">This Is A Decorated Heading<br><span class="u-font-weight--normal">And Subheading</span></h3>
